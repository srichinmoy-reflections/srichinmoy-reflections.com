Sri Chinmoy 1,000 Mile Race
Flushing Meadow Park
Queens, New York, NY  

Distance: 208 miles
Place: 10th overall
 

 

Day 4: May 4, 1985

Photo by Bhashwar Hart

 

Sri Chinmoy was an official entrant in the 1,000-mile race, which was conducted on a one-mile asphalt course at Flushing Meadow Park. And New York Road Runners Club President Fred Lebow, a frequent visitor to the race, was there at the finish to hand out the awards.
