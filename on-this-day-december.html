  DECEMBER 1ST


1970

Sri Chinmoy delivered a lecture, entitled ‘Attachment And Detachment’, at Trinity College in Dublin, Ireland.


1978

Sri Chinmoy offered a tribute programme for Eleanor Roosevelt, with guest speaker grandson Curtis Roosevelt, at the United Nations in New York.


1987
     


Sri Chinmoy offered a Peace Concert at the Sports and Entertainment Centre in Melbourne, Australia.


1987
       


Sri Chinmoy lifted a dumbbell of 73.5 kg (162 lb.), more than his own body weight of 70.5 kg (155.4 lb.), using only one arm, for the first time ever on television, in Melbourne, Australia. (The lift was pre-recorded on November 29, by the TV station following an interview on Good Morning Melbourne, but broadcast on The Today Show, on December 1.)


1993
    “Spending an evening with you is a rare privilege, a spiritually enriching experience. Not only will we not forget it, but I am sure it will nourish us for a long time.” — Karel Kovanda, Permanent Representative of the Czech Republic to the United Nations  


Sri Chinmoy lifted H.E. Karel Kovanda, Permanent Representative of the Czech Republic to the UN, in Jamaica, NY, USA.


1994

Sri Chinmoy offered a Peace Concert/New Year’s Concert at St. Paul the Apostle Church in New York, NY, USA.


1995

Sri Chinmoy offered a Peace Concert at the Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan (USA) in New York, NY, USA.


1998

Sri Chinmoy offered a Peace Concert/New Year’s Concert at Norman Thomas High School in New York, NY, USA.


2002

Sri Chinmoy completed his 14,000,000th Soul-Bird drawing in Taupo, New Zealand.


2002

Sri Chinmoy continued his project to lift 1,000 lambs by lifting 200 Perendale and Perendale-Suffolk Cross lambs (101-300) on his second day of lifting at Waitomo Farm in Taupo, New Zealand. Lifting four or six lambs at a time, the total weight was 13,405 lbs. In addition, Sri Chinmoy also lifted three of the sheep farm owners.


2004

Sri Chinmoy lifted 40 people with lifts of over 500 lbs. each, at Resort Intime in Sanya, China.


2005

Sri Chinmoy lifted 35 people at Pangkor Island Beach Resort, on Pangkor Island, Malaysia.
