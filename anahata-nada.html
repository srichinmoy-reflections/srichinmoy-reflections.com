Anahata Nada

Anahata Nada was the longest running newsletter of the Sri Chinmoy Centre. It was first published on January 1, 1974, and chronicled Sri Chinmoy’s life and activities for over three decades until 2007. From its inception it was edited by Chidananda.

The following extracts from Anahata Nada have been referenced in various articles and yearly timelines throughout the Sri Chinmoy Reflections site:
