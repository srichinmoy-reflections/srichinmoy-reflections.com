SRI CHINMOY REFLECTIONS


 
 
Sri Chinmoy (1931-2007)

This site aspires to serve as a venue wherein readers from all backgrounds may offer their reflections on the life and works of Sri Chinmoy.

Sri Chinmoy believed that spirituality is an integral part of everyday life. Within the context of his own life, he showed that the inner silence born of deep meditation can give meaning to everything we do. He was a poet, a dramatist, a storyteller, an artist, a musician, a composer, a singer, an athlete, a dreamer of peace — in sum, an extraordinary individual whose inspiration overflowed into numerous creative endeavours. He was also a spiritual master, guiding seekers in the quest for self-discovery.

In everything Sri Chinmoy did, he expressed the essence of the human spirit, and our dreams and hopes for a brighter future for all mankind.

We invite you to consult our expanding menu for biographical details about Sri Chinmoy, as well as articles offering insights into various aspects of his life.
