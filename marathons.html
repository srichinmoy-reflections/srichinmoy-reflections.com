New York, NY
 Time: 4:48:53

7. 2 December 1979

                                      Time: 4:33:55

                                      Asbury Park, New Jersey

This race completed Sri Chinmoy’s 7th marathon in his first nine months of taking up the sport.

 

 

*. 1 February 1980

                                      Time: 6:01:27
                                      Pace: 13:47
                                      Solo walking marathon
                                      Streets of Briarwood and Jamaica

Sri Chinmoy commenced walking at 1:30 a.m. His 3-mile route went along the Grand Central Service Road and continued on neighbourhood streets. He did not want this marathon to be counted among his official times.

                                      Sri Chinmoy Marathon
                                      San Mateo, California

Sri Chinmoy began his long distance running career in San Francisco, California, on 1 June 1978. This race was held in honour of the second anniversary of this most significant date.

Sri Chinmoy returned to the race where he had recorded his personal best time two years previously with the hope that he would run another fast time, but he suffered greatly from cramps from mile seventeen onwards. Read Sri Chinmoy’s account of the race.
