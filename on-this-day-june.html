Sri Chinmoy began cycling again for the first time since he came to the West. He joined his students for one circuit as they rode a course in Central Park, New York, singing his ‘O My America’ song in honour of America’s Bicentennial. Sri Chinmoy’s love of cycling dates back to his childhood in Chittagong, East Bengal, and continued during his time at the Sri Aurobindo Ashram in Pondicherry, India, where the bicycle was a common mode of transport. His cycling career began in earnest in 1977 when he and his students participated in the 24-Hour Pepsi Bicycle Marathon in Central Park, New York, NY, USA.
