1988
   

Listen to: Organ at the First United Church of Christ, Milford

 


Listen to: Organ at Woolsey Hall, Yale University, New Haven

  

Sri Chinmoy offered two pipe organ performances in Connecticut, USA. A morning recital was held at the First United Church of Christ Congregational in Milford. Sri Chinmoy then lifted his own body-weight and was interviewed on Fairfield Exchange, a Norwalk-based television show. Next came an organ recital at Woolsey Hall at Yale University in New Haven, followed by an interview with the New Haven Register. The day ended with a television interview at the college station at the University of Bridgeport. Listen to the CD...

    Prior to his first lecture in Australia, Sri Chinmoy offered his most soulful gratitude to the Dean of Perth: “This morning I had the golden opportunity to be blessed by his august presence. With all the sincerity at my command I pray to the Christ-consciousness to grant Mother Earth a few more sincere seekers and sincere lovers of mankind like the Dean of Perth, so that our planet earth can have a better peace, a more fulfilling heart and a more satisfying reality.”
